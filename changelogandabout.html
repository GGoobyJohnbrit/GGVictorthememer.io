About

GGmod.io (not to be confused with Gmod or Gary's mod) is a site that hosts embedded scratch projects with turbowarp packager, and also holds penguinmod projects, with permission from the original creator, this repo was made by the redditor u/aethelgard_of2 aka GGoobyJohnbrit.
